About Us

While living in the Kootenay Valley of beautiful British Columbia it stopped making sense to feed our dog Charlie processed treats. So we went to local farms and got left over cuts from their butchered cows. Dehydrating the locally farmed beef in our own kitchen, our dog would be stargin drooling the whole preparation! Now we would like to share our homemade dog treats with you!
